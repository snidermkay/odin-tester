Edible Penguins

Cream Cheese Penguins

You like penguins.
You love cheese.

Why not put them together and throw them in your mouth?

Ingredients
  • 18 jumbo black olives, pitted
  • (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
  • 18 small black olives
  • 1 carrot
Steps
  1. Cut a slit from top to bottom, lengthwise, into the side of each jumbo olive. Carefully insert about 1 teaspoon of cream cheese into each olive.
  2. Slice the carrot into eighteen 1/4 inch thick rounds; cut a small notch out of each carrot slice to form feet. Save the cut out piece and press into center of small olive to form the beak.
  3. If necessary cut a small slit into each olive before inserting the beak.
  4. Set a big olive, large hole side down, onto a carrot slice. Then, set a small olive onto the large olive, adjusting so that the beak, cream cheese chest and notch in the carrot slice line up.
  5. Secure with a toothpick.
